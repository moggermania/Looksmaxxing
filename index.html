Looksmaxxing Guide

Skincare

Daily cleansing, moisturizing, and sunscreen are essentials. Consider products with niacinamide and retinoids for skin improvement.

Haircare

Keep your hair clean and styled. Use products that suit your hair type, and maintain regular haircuts.

Fitness

Exercise regularly to improve posture and muscle tone. Strength training and cardio both help.

Style

Wear clothes that fit well and match your personal style. Confidence is key!
